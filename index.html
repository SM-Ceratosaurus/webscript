About Me

My name's S.M. Cerato, and I strive to know more. About plants, insects, industries, and you. I love learning the in's and out's of the world, and this is a small fraction of my path to explore. You will find my projects, passions, and notes out here in the wilderness. My content follows a variety of mediums, from the arts, to photography, to coding, to data collection and coalescence. I tidy up existing data and I make extremely niche contraptions that, if you ever needed them, you'd be using them daily like me. I hope they serve you well as they did me to discover them.
